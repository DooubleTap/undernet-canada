Channel Rules

We are a bilingual channel (English & Français). Respect everyone!

  1. No harassment or hate speech.
  2. Respect both English and French speakers.
  3. No spamming or flooding.
  4. Keep discussions civil and on-topic.
  5. No illegal content or activities.
  6. Respect operators and their decisions.
  7. Do not share personal information without consent.
  8. Links must be safe and relevant.
  9. Use common sense and be kind.
