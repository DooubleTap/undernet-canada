About #Canada

#Canada has been around on Undernet since the early days of IRC. The channel was created to provide Canadians and friends of Canada with a place to gather, discuss culture, politics, technology, and just relax.

Over the years, we’ve grown into a welcoming bilingual community where both English and French speakers are equally at home. Our goal is to keep the spirit of IRC alive while offering a friendly place to connect with others.
